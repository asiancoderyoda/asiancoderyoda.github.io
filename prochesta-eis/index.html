Features

A few features to take a glance for why you should choose guidance with us

Classroom sessions

With an innovative and strategic approach, Our classroom sessions develop student’s
analytical skills and problem-solving abilities which is an essential ingredient to achieve success in any competitive examination.

Daily Practice Sheet

Under this plan, student is given 8-10 questions in every class. These problems put emphasis on application of fundamentals and concepts. As these questions clarify even the slightest of doubts, thus student become conceptually very sound. DPPS ignites student thinking process and improves temperament to face new and challenging problems with speed, accuracy and confidence.

Problem Counter

We have developed a unique concept of “Problem Counter”. If an individual student feels that a particular problem/statement/theory is not clear, then he/she may clarify doubts at Problem Counter in the one-to-one interaction mode.

Doubt Classes

For each and every topic, we conduct Doubt Removal Classes regularly after completion of the theory of that topic. Our faculty members clarify doubts of students to the root level in order to ensure complete preparation in all respect.

Exercise Sheets

Along with comprehensive study material, we also provide Exercise Sheets to students as home assignments. These exercise sheets are designed after thorough research and analysis of the latest examination pattern of different competitive examinations.

Study Material

During the course, we provide complete up to date study material for JEXPO, VOCLET, WBJEE, JELET, JEE (Main), JEE (Advanced), JEEDEC, NATA, etc. This study material has been prepared by our 
experienced subject experts.The study material is self sufficient in all respect; therefore reference to any other material is not required at all. Study material is available in English or Bengali medium.

Scheduled test series

We conduct scheduled periodic test series based on the latest pattern of respective examination with similar test atmosphere. Regular test series increases speed, accuracy, tune student to actual examination conditions and develops competitive spirit in order to enable each student to meet success.

Career Counselling

We conduct individual and group counseling & seminars for our students. 
These counseling and seminar sessions are usually organized with the sole objective to prepare our students to face the challenges with enough confidence
and competence. 

Qualified Faculty

The faculty team comprises of highly qualified, knowledgeable and experienced faculty members for each subject, the faculty members at Vidya-Guru always encourage students to raise their doubts,
so that students could have sound understanding of the subjects.
